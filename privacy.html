info 1. Introduction

Welcome to MindFrix ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy governs our data collection, processing, and usage practices when you visit our website mindfrix.com, use our AI agents, chatbot integration tools, custom platforms, or otherwise interact with our services.

By using our website or services, you consent to the data practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the practices described here, you should immediately discontinue your use of our site and systems.

database 2. Data Collection

We collect information to provide intelligent business infrastructure and improve our service offerings. The types of data we collect include:

A. Personal Information You Provide

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you fill out contact forms, schedule consultations, subscribe to newsletters, or engage with our online support chatbots. This may include:

  • First and last name
  • Email address and telephone number
  • Business name and industry
  • Any information you input during chat conversations with our automated customer service agents

B. Automatically Collected Information

When you browse our website, our servers automatically collect details about your device and browser. This standard diagnostic data includes:

  • Internet Protocol (IP) address
  • Browser type, language, and operating system
  • Referring URL, timestamp, and clicked elements
  • Performance logs and page-load speeds

psychology 3. Usage of Data

MindFrix uses the collected data for various operational, analytical, and improvements tasks:

  • Deliver and Improve Services: To build, operate, maintain, and optimize our AI-driven automation pipelines and digital custom solutions.
  • Client Communication: To reply directly to your questions, process requests, deliver support alerts, and share informational or marketing updates.
  • AI Training & Accuracy: Conversations handled by our AI chatbots are analyzed to train models, enhance responses, and ensure reliable conversational performance.
  • Operational Analytics: To diagnose technological bugs, secure websites, identify usage trends, and measure marketing impact.

verified_user 4. Security

The security of your data is of critical importance to us. We employ industry-standard administrative, physical, and technical safeguards designed to protect personal data from accidental loss, destruction, alteration, or unauthorized access.

However, please be aware that no security measure is 100% impenetrable. While we strive to protect your personal data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security during transmission over the internet or when stored in electronic hosting systems.

share 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We work with trusted vendor partners who provide cloud database services, analytics, payment processing, or email delivery. These vendors are strictly bound by confidentiality contracts.
  • Compliance with Law: We may disclose data if legally required to do so by public authorities, courts, or government regulatory bodies.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, restructure, or asset sale, your personal information may be transferred as part of the business assets.

rule 6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have specific data rights under local privacy regulations (such as GDPR, CCPA, or DPDP Act). These rights include:

  • Access and Export: You can request a summary and a portable copy of the personal information we maintain about you.
  • Rectification: You can request that we correct incomplete, outdated, or inaccurate details.
  • Deletion ("Right to be Forgotten"): You can request that we securely delete all your personal data from our databases, subject to certain legal limits.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt-out of marketing communications at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in our emails or contacting our team.

cookie 7. Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies (like web beacons and local storage) to provide essential website performance, compile anonymous site traffic analytics, and save your visual preferences.

You can choose to block or disable cookies via your browser configurations. However, doing so may disable or disrupt certain functional structures and interactive features on our website.
